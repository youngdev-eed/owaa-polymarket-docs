## Overview

Depositing directly to Polymarket from Coinbase is simple and easy. This guide walks you through the process of purchasing USDC on Coinbase and transferring it to your Polymarket account.

## Buying USDC on Coinbase

First, you'll need to purchase USDC on Coinbase. If you need help creating a Coinbase account, refer to Coinbase's official setup documentation.

## Transferring to Polymarket

Once you've purchased USDC on Coinbase, follow these steps to transfer it to your Polymarket account:

1. Log into your Coinbase account
2. Navigate to your USDC wallet
3. Click "Send" or "Transfer"
4. Enter your Polymarket deposit address
5. Confirm the transaction

Your USDC will be transferred to your Polymarket account.

## Alternative Deposit Methods

Polymarket provides links to alternative deposit options, including:
- Transferring crypto directly
- Using credit/debit cards through MoonPay

## Troubleshooting

If something went wrong along the way, we recommend reaching out to Coinbase support for assistance with your Coinbase transaction, and contacting Polymarket support if you need help with the deposit process.
