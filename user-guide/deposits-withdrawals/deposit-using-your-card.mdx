## Overview

Polymarket enables users to deposit funds using credit cards or bank accounts through MoonPay integration. This is a convenient way to purchase USDC directly without needing to use a cryptocurrency exchange.

## Supported Payment Methods

MoonPay accepts the following payment methods:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- Bank account transfers

## How to Deposit

1. Click the "Buy USDC" button on Polymarket's deposit page
2. You'll be directed to the MoonPay interface
3. Select your preferred payment method
4. Enter the amount of USDC you want to purchase
5. Complete the transaction through MoonPay's secure interface

Your USDC will be automatically transferred to your Polymarket account once the transaction is confirmed.

## Additional Resources

For detailed instructions on using MoonPay, please refer to MoonPay's guide in their support documentation.

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues during the deposit process, you can:
- Review MoonPay's support documentation
- Contact Polymarket support via live chat on Polymarket.com
- Email the Polymarket support team for assistance
