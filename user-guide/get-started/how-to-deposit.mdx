# How to Deposit

Polymarket offers multiple flexible ways to fund your account. All transactions operate in USDC (USD Coin).

## Deposit Methods

### 1. Crypto Transfer

Deposit by transferring cryptocurrency directly to your Polymarket wallet. This method is ideal if you already hold crypto assets.

### 2. Coinbase Integration

Connect your Coinbase account for a seamless deposit experience. You can transfer funds directly from your Coinbase account.

### 3. Card Payments

Use your credit or debit card to purchase USDC through MoonPay. This is the most convenient option if you don't have existing cryptocurrency.

**Process:**
- Deposit Using Your Card
- Complete the MoonPay payment flow
- Funds are immediately available for trading

### 4. USDC on Ethereum

If you already hold USDC on the Ethereum network, you can deposit it directly to your Polymarket account.

### 5. Large Cross-Chain Deposits

For substantial transfers across multiple blockchain networks, Polymarket supports large deposits from various blockchain networks.

## Currency

All transactions on Polymarket operate in USDC (USD Coin). Every pair of event outcomes is fully collateralized by $1.00 USDC, establishing this stablecoin as the platform's standard settlement currency.

## Deposit Security

Your deposits are secure and backed by smart contracts. USDC is a regulated stablecoin maintained by Centre, providing transparency and security.

## Withdrawal

Once you've completed trades, you can withdraw your funds using the reverse of your deposit method. Withdrawals typically process quickly, depending on your chosen method.

After depositing funds, you're ready to:
