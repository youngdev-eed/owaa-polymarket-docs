# How to Sign Up

Getting started on Polymarket is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account.

## Sign-Up Process

To begin trading on Polymarket, you'll need to create an account by visiting the Polymarket platform and following the account creation process.

## What You'll Need

Before signing up, ensure you have:

- A valid email address
- Access to a device with internet connectivity
- If depositing funds, a payment method (crypto wallet, bank card, or Coinbase account)

## After Sign-Up

Once you've created your account, you'll be able to:

- Buy and sell shares representing future event outcomes
- Deposit funds through various methods
- Make your first trade on prediction markets
- Browse and participate in thousands of prediction markets

## Next Steps

After successfully signing up:

1. **Deposit funds** - Add USDC to your account using your preferred payment method
2. **Browse markets** - Explore the available prediction markets
3. **Make your first trade** - Buy or sell shares on an outcome you believe in

## Support

If you encounter any issues during the sign-up process, refer to our FAQs or contact our support team for assistance.
