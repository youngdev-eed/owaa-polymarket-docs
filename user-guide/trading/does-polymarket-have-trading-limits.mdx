## No Hard Trading Size Restrictions

The Polymarket orderbook is designed without trading size restrictions. The platform matches willing buyers and sellers for any amount of shares. However, traders should understand that large transactions may significantly impact prices depending on available liquidity.

## Important Consideration

Before executing trades—particularly substantial ones—users should examine the orderbook to assess liquidity depth. This means checking how many market participants exist and what prices they're willing to trade at.

## Key Takeaway

Polymarket operates as a peer-to-peer trading platform without hard position limits. Success depends on finding counterparties willing to trade at desired prices and volumes. The platform emphasizes transparency through orderbook visibility, enabling informed trading decisions.
