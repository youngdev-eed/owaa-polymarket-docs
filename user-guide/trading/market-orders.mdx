Once you've signed up and deposited funds, you're ready to start trading on Polymarket.

## Placing a Market Order

Before trading, you'll want to visit the markets page to find a market that interests you.

### Steps to trade:

1. **Choose a market** - Browse available prediction markets
2. **Buy shares** - Select the outcome you want to trade on and enter the amount
3. **Share your bet slip** - Confirm your trade and optionally share it with others

## Next Steps

Once you've mastered market orders, you can learn about more advanced trading and order types. Check out the [Limit Orders](/user-guide/trading/limit-orders) guide to explore additional trading strategies.
